Welcome to the Molesey Beer Festival

Join us for great local beers and Oktoberfest inspired food. Families welcome on Saturday afternoon and live music both evenings.
The Molesey Beer Festival is a volunteer run community event raising funds for 1st Molesey Scouts.

FAQs

Can I buy tickets on the door?

Tickets are not available at the door, please purchase online before the event.

Will there be children's entertainment?

There will be a few simple activities to occupy children but we are not providing children’s entertainment.

What happens if it rains?

We have lots of cover outside and room inside. There's plenty of of space so we look forward to welcoming as many of you as possible.
