For any Surrey-based cider lovers out there, make sure you don't miss this! The Garden Cider Company will be bringing along their brilliant range of sustainable ciders for you to try in October. Formed by Ben & Will Filby, the Chiddingfold-based brewery makes a range of hand-crafted premium ciders, sourced entirely from local, donated garden apples.
